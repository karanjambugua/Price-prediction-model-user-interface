Research & Technology

Our price prediction model is built on advanced **machine learning techniques** and trained on diverse datasets spanning multiple product categories.

We use a Random Forest regression model to capture complex relationships between product attributes and prices. Its ensemble approach reduces overfitting and improves accuracy.

Features such as product category, brand, condition, and market location are preprocessed using scaling, encoding, and missing-value handling.

The model achieved an R² score of 0.992, indicating high accuracy. We also validate using Mean Squared Error (MSE) and cross-validation techniques.
